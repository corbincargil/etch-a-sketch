Instructions:

  1. Click and drag the mouse over the grid to paint.
  2. Use the controls above to change the grid size, pick a color, and toggle the eraser.
  3. When you are done painting, you can click the "Clear" button to clear the canvas.

Tips:

  • To decrease the size of the pixels, slide the Grid Size slider to the right
  • Keep in mind that changing the grid size will erase your current work.

Enjoy!
