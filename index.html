Pixel Art Sketching

Instructions:

  1. Click and drag the mouse over the grid to paint.
  2. If you wish to restart and use a different number of rows and columns, use the controls below.
  3. When you are done painting, you can click the "Clear" button to clear the canvas.

Tips:

  • To decrease the size of the pixels, use a higher number of rows/columns
  • If the canvas is not fitting inside your browser window, try changing the width of the browser window to adjust the size of the canvas

Enjoy!
